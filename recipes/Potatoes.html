Mashed Potatoes

Instructions

    Ingredients
  • Potatoes
  • Sour Cream
  • Cream cheese
  • Milk
  • Onion salt
  • Black Pepper

      • How To Make Mashed potatoes Step by Step
  • Transfer potatoes into a large bowl and mash.
  • Let potatoes cool completely
  • remove casserole from the refrigerator
  • Bake, covered, in the preheated oven for 50 minutes.
